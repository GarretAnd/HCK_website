Hanover Community Kitchen
Welcome to the Hanover Community Kitchen Pantry! Scroll around or use the menu in the top to navigate.
The Hanover Community Kitchen strives to create community across the dinner table by preparing weekly meals using donated supplies for local residents. Our goal is to build community in the Hanover area, foster culinary and interpersonal skills, and encourage service to others.
Hanover Community Kitchen is a student run non-for-profit that relies completely on the generosity of the community. If you can spare, please consider donating and making a difference in the lives of others.
Want to get involved with Hanover Community Kitchen? Feel free to stop by or donate supplies to Our Savior Lutheran Church and Campus Ministry at 5 Summer St, Hanover, NH 03755. All volunteers are welcome, we love the extra hands! If you don't know how to cook don't worry, we love teaching!
